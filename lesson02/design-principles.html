White Space and Clean Design

Pocket Penguins

Pocket Penguins

Pocket Penguins is a series of books that is known for their covers, and minimalistic style choices, including bold colors and bold text, that make them stand out in any place. This attribute is reflected on their website, where they make use of the blank space to put both the brand and the books on the spotlight.

Hick's Law

Netflix

Netflix

Netflix is an online streaming service that is used by people all around the world to watch TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more. Having such a huge library of movies and series, they make the task of choosing what to watch much easier for their users by grouping them in categories and showing the top most watched shows first. Then later it will also generate categories tailored to their user based on watch history.

Fitts's Law

Airbnb

Airbnb

Airbnb is a short-term rental website, targeted towards vacation. Their aim is to make it as easy as possible for travelers to connect with property owners. This can be seen in their website. The search box is big and at the top, surrounded by all the relevant options. The rentals are the main focus, easily accessible in the middle of the screen, ensuring that the user doesn't have to move much to traverse the website.
