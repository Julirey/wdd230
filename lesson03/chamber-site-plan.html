Overview

Site Name

Maracaibo Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Maracaibo Chamber of Commerce helps promoting business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people. We aim to act as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion that vouches for its economic growth.

Scenarios

A President/CEO of a company from another city wants to create communications and build relationships with the Maracaibo chamber of commerce to propose collaborations that could benefit both cities and help expand businesses and give birth to new opportunities for future collaborations. So he might need to know what the Maracaibo chamber of commerce looks like, their members, and general contact information to possibly arrange meetings.

A small business owner wants to see if they could get help from the chamber of commerce to meet with other local business owners and provide sources to grow his network. As well as maybe have their business presented in a positive light to the community. He might need the contact information of the member that handles communications between local businesses, and would probably also like to know how to plan meetings or take part into events.

A local resident wishes to revitalize the part of the city where he lives. He might need to know which members of the chamber of commerce he should talk to inform investors of possible businesses that could create new jobs and advertisements that promote it and attract people.
